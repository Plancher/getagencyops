The 4-Step Fix

01

Find the waste

We map your workflows, measure time loss, and compile a leak report with baseline metrics for onboarding speed, manual steps, show rate, failed payments, and reporting time.

02

Fix the flow

We cut the fluff and build automations across onboarding, delivery handoffs, reporting, and alerts. Tools connected, data deduped, lifecycle statuses enforced.

03

Make it steady

We launch and monitor with Early Warning alerts for budget pacing, conversion drops, and missed deadlines. Alerts routed to owners, thresholds documented, SOPs linked.

04

Keep it running

Full documentation, change log, and 60–90 minute team training. One revision round per department. Optional maintenance to keep systems healthy.
