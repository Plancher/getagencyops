The System

How It Works

Most agency reporting tools show you what happened. This tells you what to do about it.

01

Connect Your Inputs

Enter your client's core numbers once. Average customer value. Target CAC. Close rate. Gross margin. Every metric, risk flag, and threshold runs off these inputs automatically — no manual recalculation, ever again.

02

Build Your Control Board

Ad spend. Leads. Booked appointments. Show rate. Close rate. Cost per customer. ROAS. All pulled into one connected sheet. When a client asks "is this working?" — you open the board. The answer is already there.

03

Activate Risk Scoring

Every account gets a live risk level — Low, Medium, or High. The system flags CAC pressure, close rate drops, channel dependency, and margin compression before your client notices. You stop reacting to churn — you start catching it at the signal stage.

04

Embed Decision Logic

CPL rising but close rate stable? Creative issue. CPL stable but close rate dropping? Sales issue. CAC within target and margin strong? Scale trigger. The system converts data into action categories — Scale, Fix, Stabilize, Diversify — so your team always knows the next move.

05

Hand Clients a Business Story

One filtered tab. Customers acquired. Cost per customer. Revenue generated. Growth vs. prior period. Risk summary. The shift from "we generated 47 leads" to "you acquired 9 customers at $312 cost with stable margin" is the difference between a vendor they tolerate and a partner they protect.
